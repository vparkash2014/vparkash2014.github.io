Background & Networks

Monash University

In 2019, I completed my PhD thesis in Observational Astrophysics. My research focused on the neutral hydrogen content and star formation properties of local galaxies to gain insight on galaxy evolution. As part of international collaboration, I successful written six proposals to obtain time on professional telescopes and published two first-author scientific papers. My thesis titled "Star formation within nearby galaxies."

Fulbright Program

In 2015, I was recieved an Fulbright Student Fellowship. I spent 10 months between University of Göttingen, Germany and Aarhus University, Denmark to study exoplanets. I developed a Python pipeline to reduce and analyze the observations of possible transits of extrasolar planets.

Union College

In 2014, I graduated with a BSc with Departmental Honors in Physics with minors in Mathematics and Astrophysics. As part of my study aboard program, I interned at CTIO in Chile to study observation astronomy and galaxy evolution.
